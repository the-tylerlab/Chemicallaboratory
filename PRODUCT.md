# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Laboratory administrators (Admins), teachers, and students. Users need to manage laboratory items, chemical inventory, schedule rooms, book slots, request borrowings, and process purchase orders in a high-density, high-efficiency environment.

## Product Purpose
To manage chemical inventory, lab room bookings, and scientific equipment library flows in a reliable, modern, and high-efficiency system. Success means frictionless bookings, clear stock status, and reliable data persistence.

## Brand Personality
Modern, high-tech, vibrant, and trustworthy. The UI should feel clean and readable but modern (vibrant purple/blue color scheme, sleek cards, responsive, subtle micro-animations).

## Anti-references
- Low contrast layouts (e.g. light gray text on light background).
- SaaS dashboard clichés like overused gradients, identical card grids with tiny uppercase eyebrows, and nested cards.
- Side-stripe borders and cluttered spacing.

## Design Principles
- **Readability & Density First**: Prioritize high readability (good contrast, prompt font, clear labels) with dense, functional tables and forms.
- **Role-Aware Workflows**: Clearly distinguish teacher/admin management options (quick buttons, status updates, edit modes) from student views.
- **Reliability & Alignment**: Grid borders, form inputs, and stat cards must align precisely to build visual trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body copy and readable form fields.
- Reduced motion support (prefers-reduced-motion media query).
- Clean semantic HTML structure.
